About

This website aims to raise awareness on the situation of STEM in the Philippines through the use of articles that summarize content from various verified sources.

siSTEMa wishes to highlight that the problems the STEM industry is facing right now is not something we can address individually but rather systemically. As you explore the website, you'll find:

  • Organizations in the Philippines that can provide you with more in-depth information on specific STEM issues such as gender inequality in the tech industry or climate action
  • Short but adequate information on STEM education in the Philippines and why it matters

It is recommended that you read the articles from left to right based on how they are presented in the home page. (importance first, then challenges, and improving STEM education last).

After you explore every nook and cranny of siSTEMa, I hope it not only empowers you to push for change in STEM education but also allows you to appreciate STEM as a subject.
